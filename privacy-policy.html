What We DO NOT Collect

✅ We NEVER store your customers' transaction data, account numbers, or personal financial information.

Specifically, FinRaksha AI does NOT collect or store:

  • Your bank customers' names, Aadhaar numbers, or PAN cards
  • Account numbers or IFSC codes sent via API
  • Actual transaction amounts or balances after processing
  • UPI IDs or mobile numbers of end customers
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII) of third parties
  • Passwords or authentication tokens

06

Zero Data Retention Policy (API)

This is our most important commitment to banks:

✅ ZERO DATA RETENTION: Transaction data sent to our API is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We do not log, store, or cache any transaction-level data after returning the fraud score.

Our API works like this:

  1. Bank sends transaction data via POST request
  2. Our XGBoost model processes it in memory (<200ms)
  3. Fraud score + SHAP explanation is returned
  4. All transaction data is discarded immediately
  5. Only API call metadata (timestamp, response time) is logged for uptime monitoring

This means your customers' financial data never leaves your control permanently.

RBI Compliance

FinRaksha AI is designed to help banks comply with RBI's fraud monitoring guidelines:

  • Aligned with RBI Master Direction on Fraud — Classification and Reporting (2016)
  • Supports RBI Circular RBI/2021-22/34 on digital payment security controls
  • Every fraud alert generates an audit-ready report with timestamp, transaction ID, fraud score, and SHAP explanation
  • Data processing is compliant with the Indian IT Act 2000 and IT (Amendment) Act 2008
  • Designed to support banks' obligations under Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA)

📋 We provide full audit trail exports on request — ready for RBI inspection without any additional work on your team's part.
